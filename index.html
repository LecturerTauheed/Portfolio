About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated Lecturer in Computer Science currently serving in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Higher Education Department since 2024. I am committed to shaping the future of technology by teaching and mentoring undergraduate students in computer science subjects such as programming, web development, and software engineering.

With a deep understanding of both academic and practical aspects of computer science, I bring a unique blend of teaching, technical expertise, and real-world problem-solving. I am also skilled in curriculum design, academic advising, and integrating innovative teaching methods using technology.
